## Top Recommended Datasets

Several large-scale, high-quality datasets provide the exact schema you need—including direct image links, product descriptions, unique identifiers (ASINs/SKUs), and customer reviews. [1, 2] 

* Amazon Berkeley Objects (ABO) Dataset: This is a professional-grade collection specifically released by Amazon. It includes 147,702 product listings with multilingual metadata (title, brand, material) and 398,212 high-quality catalog images.
* [McAuley Lab Amazon Review Data (2023)](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/wajahat1064/amazon-reviews-data-2023/versions/2): One of the most comprehensive academic sources for Amazon data. It contains millions of reviews, ratings, and product metadata including descriptions, price, and raw image URLs.
* [Amazon E-commerce Products & Reviews (Kaggle)](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/lazylad99/amazon-e-commerce-product-and-review-dataset): A "ready-to-use" version ideal for demos. It features detailed product metadata like brand name, model number (SKU/ASIN equivalent), direct image URLs, and sentiment-scored reviews.
* [SNAP Amazon Product Data](https://snap.stanford.edu/data/amazon/productGraph/): A classic large-scale dataset containing 143.7 million reviews from 1996 to 2014, including descriptions, price, and category information. [3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8] 

## Comparison of Data Fields

| Dataset [4, 6, 8, 9, 10] | Total Products | Key Fields Provided | Best Use Case |
|---|---|---|---|
| ABO Dataset | 147K+ | Images, Dimensions, 3D Models, Materials | Visual-heavy store fronts |
| McAuley (2023) | Millions | Reviews, Descriptions, Image Links, Price | Recommendation engines |
| Kaggle (2025) | 42K+ (Electronics) | Sales, Pricing Trends, Ratings | Analytics-focused demos |
| Datahive AI | Sample-based | Breadcrumbs, ASIN, Alt-Images, Ratings | Fast prototype/Mockups |

## Where to Download

* Kaggle: Best for pre-cleaned CSV files that are easy to import into a database like PostgreSQL.
* [AWS Marketplace (Free Samples)](https://aws.amazon.com/marketplace/pp/prodview-w6cuvuwu6qrlo): Offers free samples of high-resolution images and metadata specifically for AWS users.
* [Hugging Face Datasets](https://huggingface.co/datasets/datahiveai/Amazon-Reviews-Dataset): Excellent for streaming data directly into your app without downloading massive files. [11, 12, 13, 14]
